Instructions


Thank you for choosing to participate in this experiment!

You are asked to participate in an online game in which there are two players: a “worker” and a “fetcher”. The goal of the worker is to go to the correct numbered station. The goal of the fetcher to go to the toolbox, obtain the corresponding tool, and then go to the worker’s station.

A given scenario will look similar to the image on the right

The start of a given scenario

In this scenario, we see 4 stations labelled with numbers 1 to 4. These stations are possible places where the worker can go. The worker’s goal is shown as the green station (station 4 in this scenario).

The fetcher does not know the worker's goal and will need to infer this goal based on the worker's actions. It will go to the toolbox, where it will need to choose the right tool that corresponds to the worker's goal workstation, and then fetch that tool to the worker.
Worker on top of the goal station; press “Done” at this stage.

You will play the role of the worker: the circular yellow agent, labelled “W”. Your goal is to move to the goal station and press the “Done” button once you’ve reached it. Your main objective should be to act in such a way that the fetcher can figure out what you goal is at the earliest possible timestep. That way, the fetcher will know as soon as possible what tool to fetch. There will be 8 different scenarios. Each scenario will have a different layout and a different goal station, so make sure to go to the correct goal station for the given scenario.

These are the controls:

Tab - Pause/Unpause
Up - Move up
Left - Move left
Down - Move down
Right - Move right
Space - Done (press when you arrive at the green station)
Enter - Stop (stop movement for one turn)

Each scenario will start on the Pause menu, where you can view the goal station and the controls, press Tab to change to the experiment screen. If you forget the goal station, or any of the controls, press Tab again to view the controls.

You will start with 2 practice games. These games will show you that you DO NOT need to go to the tool station. Also, you are able to go through other stations.


Good luck!
